<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Retrieves the status of a trade using the provided trade hash. Logs the trade hash being checked. Responds with a JSON containing the trade hash and its status.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="A unique string representing the hash of the trade for which the status is being queried.">
  Hash to identify the trade.
</ParamField>
