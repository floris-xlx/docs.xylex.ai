<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches the status of a trade based on a provided trade hash. Logs the trade hash being checked and retrieves the status from the database. Responds with a JSON containing the trade hash and its status.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="The 'trade_hash' parameter serves as a unique identifier for the trade, allowing the system to fetch the trade's status from the database.">
  Hash identifier for the trade.
</ParamField>
