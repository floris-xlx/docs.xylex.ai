<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches the status of a trade using a provided trade hash. Logs the trade hash being checked. Responds with the trade hash and its current status as JSON.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="The trade_hash is a unique identifier used to look up the current status of a specific trade in the system. It is required to fetch the trade status.">
  The hash that uniquely identifies a trade.
</ParamField>
