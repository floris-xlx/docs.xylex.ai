<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches and returns the status of a trade using a provided trade hash. Logs the trade hash being checked. Responds with the trade hash and its status in JSON format.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier for the trade, represented as a string, used to fetch and verify the status of a specific trade.">
  The hash of the trade to check status.
</ParamField>
