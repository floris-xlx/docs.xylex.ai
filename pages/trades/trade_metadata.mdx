<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches trade metadata based on a provided trade hash. Logs the trade hash being queried. Returns a JSON response with the trade`s status and metadata.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier used to fetch the metadata associated with a specific trade from the database.">
  The hash identifying the trade.
</ParamField>
