<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches trade metadata using a trade hash, logs the operation, and returns the metadata with a status of `Healthy` if data is found, otherwise `Unhealthy`.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier for the trade, represented as a hash, used to fetch its metadata.">
  The hash of the trade.
</ParamField>
