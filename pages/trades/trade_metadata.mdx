<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches trade metadata based on a provided trade hash. Returns a JSON response with the trade hash, its metadata, and a status indicating whether the data is `Healthy` or `Unhealthy`.</Card>





<ParamField query="trade_hash" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier for the trade, used to retrieve specific metadata associated with it from the database.">
  The hash of the trade to fetch metadata for.
</ParamField>
