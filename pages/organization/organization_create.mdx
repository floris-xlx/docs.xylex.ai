<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Creates a new organization name by checking its uniqueness and adds it to the database, returning the organization name and creation status.</Card>





<ParamField query="organization_name" type="String" required  children="Specifies the name that the organization desires to use, which must be unique.">
  Name of the organization.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="organization_id" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier corresponding to the organization for which the name is being created.">
  Identifier for the organization.
</ParamField>
