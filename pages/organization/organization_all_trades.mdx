<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches trade metadata for a specified organization ID, returning the data along with a health status of `Healthy` or `Unhealthy` based on the presence of the trade metadata.</Card>





<ParamField query="organization_id" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier for the organization whose trade metadata is being fetched. It is used to query the database for the relevant trade information.">
  The ID representing the organization.
</ParamField>
