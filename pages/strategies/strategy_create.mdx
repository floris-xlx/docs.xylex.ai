---
title: 'Name Create'
api: 'POST https://api.v2.xylex.ai/strategy/name_create'
---



<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Creates a unique strategy name for an organization using provided strategy name, strategy ID, and organization ID. Returns a JSON response indicating the strategy name and whether it was successfully created.</Card>





<ParamField query="strategy_name" type="String" required  children="The strategy_name parameter is a unique identifier for the strategy within the organization and is used to ensure that the strategy can be distinguished from others.">
  The name of the strategy to be created.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="strategy_id" type="String" required  children="The strategy_id parameter is used to uniquely identify the strategy within the system, allowing for operations specific to this strategy.">
  The unique identifier of the strategy.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="organization_id" type="String" required  children="The organization_id parameter specifies which organization the strategy belongs to, ensuring that the strategy is associated with the correct organizational entity.">
  The unique identifier of the organization.
</ParamField>
