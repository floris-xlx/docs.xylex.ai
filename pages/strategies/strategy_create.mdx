title: 'Name Create'
api: 'POST https://api.v2.xylex.ai/strategy/name_create'
---



<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Creates a unique strategy name for an organization using provided strategy and organization IDs, and checks if it`s unique before adding to the database.</Card>





<ParamField query="strategy_name" type="String" required  children="This parameter represents the name of the strategy to be created. It must be unique within the organization.">
  The name of the strategy.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="strategy_id" type="String" required  children="This parameter is the unique identifier for the strategy being created. It is used for identification purposes within the system.">
  The ID of the strategy.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="organization_id" type="String" required  children="This parameter refers to the unique identifier for the organization under which the strategy is being created. It ensures the strategy is correctly attributed to the organization.">
  The ID of the organization.
</ParamField>
