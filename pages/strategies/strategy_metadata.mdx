---
title: 'Fetch Strategy Metadata By Strategy Id'
api: 'GET https://api.v2.xylex.ai/strategies/fetch_strategy_metadata_by_strategy_id'
---



<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches strategy metadata based on organization ID, returning the metadata and a status of `Healthy` or `Unhealthy` depending on the data`s presence.</Card>





<ParamField query="strategy_id" type="String" required  children="This parameter is used to fetch specific metadata associated with a given strategy by its unique identifier.">
  The unique identifier for the strategy.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="organization" type="String" required  children="This parameter is used to fetch metadata associated with strategies by a specific organization's identifier.">
  The name of the organization.
</ParamField>
