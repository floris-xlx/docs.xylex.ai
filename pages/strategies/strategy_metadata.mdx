<Card title="Endpoint Description" icon="info" horizontal >Fetches strategy metadata by a given strategy ID, checks the data`s availability, and returns the metadata along with a status indicating `Healthy` or `Unhealthy` based on the data`s presence.</Card>





<ParamField query="strategy_id" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier to fetch metadata for a specific strategy.">
  The ID of the strategy.
</ParamField>
<ParamField query="organization" type="String" required  children="A unique identifier for the organization to fetch strategy metadata.">
  The organization identifier.
</ParamField>
